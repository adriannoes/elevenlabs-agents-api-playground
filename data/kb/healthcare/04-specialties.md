# Healthcare demo — Specialty map (demo reference)

Fictional list to help the agent suggest an **organizational next step**, not medical treatment.

| Suggested area | When to use in this sandbox |
| --- | --- |
| General Internal Medicine | Nonspecific symptoms; follow-up of mild stable complaints |
| Cardiology | Complaints aligned with ongoing cardiology care or chest pain already clarified by a clinician |
| Pulmonology | Prolonged respiratory symptoms or suspected post-infection respiratory complications |
| Neurology | Recurrent headaches affecting quality of life already discussed as outpatient by a clinician |

Fictional network business hours: Monday–Friday, 08:00–18:00; 24/7 emergency access only where the fictional contract requires — **do not invent real phone numbers** in this demo.

For **high fever for two days** with systemic symptoms, prefer brief in-person assessment in **General Internal Medicine** or internal routing to **Pulmonology** when the history is respiratory-led and consistent with the caller’s story.
