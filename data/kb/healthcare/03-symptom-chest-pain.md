# Healthcare demo — Chest pain: safety first

This file covers initial triage when the user mentions **chest pain**, **pressure**, or **retrosternal discomfort**.

Chest pain can range from benign causes to cardiovascular emergencies. In this demo, any **new** non-trivial chest pain radiating to the arm or jaw, with cold sweats, severe nausea, or syncope should be treated as **highest priority**: instruct the caller to contact local emergency services and seek in-person care **immediately**. The agent must not diagnose or reassure in ways that minimize cardiovascular risk.

For mild atypical chest pain in a context **already** assessed by a cardiologist with stable documented findings in this fictional scenario, continuity in **Cardiology** or **General Internal Medicine** may be suggested, with the reminder that sudden worsening overrides prior guidance.

Never prescribe medications or doses in this demonstration flow. Record only high-level guidance and routing aligned with the fictional network’s policies.
