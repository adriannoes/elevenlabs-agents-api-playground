# Healthcare demo — LGPD and sensitive health data (demo)

This summarizes **fictional** privacy postures for the sandbox health scenario; it does not replace real legal policies or a DPIA.

Health data receives strengthened protection under the **Brazilian LGPD** (Law No. 13,709/2018, Art. 11). In voice or chat conversations, minimize repeating full CPF, medical-record numbers, or detailed diagnoses. **This sandbox triage line does not authenticate patients by CPF**; scheduling exercises use a fictional internal token such as **VITA-1001** plus a morning or afternoon window, not government identifiers stored in this repo.

**Retention**: when Zero Retention Mode is enabled on the ElevenLabs platform for this demo agent, treat it as reduced operational persistence for illustration — it does not guarantee absolute anonymity or regulatory compliance by itself.

**Data-subject rights in this sandbox**: fictional patients may ask about processing purposes; route formal requests to a human channel when asked.

Purposes here are **demonstration** triage and routing only; there is no automated clinical decision or substitution for a licensed professional.

Use neutral language and avoid baking sensitive data into fixed examples in public source code.
